4 January 2019
  • What's on the platform
  •  Quixxi Connect is crypto platform which allows users to transfer Ethereum and other ERC20 tokens
  •  It also Allows users to send crypto using their Gmail addresses
  •  As soon as you sign up, we create your wallet and you can customize it according to your wish
  •  Dashboard allows you to keep a track of your wallet. It also displays the list of all the transactions.
  •  My Wallet shows the credit available in your wallet.
  •  Settings, allows users to create a backup of their private key and provides other services
  •  Search, allows users to search other users available on the platform
